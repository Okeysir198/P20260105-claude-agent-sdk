## Usage Guidelines

### Pre-Legal Stage (120-150+ Days)
- Use appropriate discount percentage based on balance and days overdue
- Ratio 4 = Pre-Legal department handling
- 150+ Days = Extended pre-legal process
- Higher balances (>R5K) get maximum discount for category

### Legal/Booksale Stage (150+ Days with Attorney)
- Legal = Attorney-handled accounts
- Booksale = Final collection phase
- Maximum discount percentages to encourage settlement
- Legal fees may be added to balance calculations
