PRE-LEGAL & LEGAL 150+ SCRIPT 
1. Introduction
“Good day, you are speaking to {Agent’s Name} and I am calling you from Viljoen Attorneys on behalf of Cartrack. May I speak to {Client’s Full Name}.”

2. Verification (POPI)
“Please note that this call is recorded for quality and security purposes & to ensure I am speaking to the right person, please confirm the following details:” 
    • Confirm any 3 of the following:
    a) Username
    b) Date of birth
    c) Vehicle registration
    d) Vehicle Make
    e) Vehicle Model
    f) Vehicle colour
    g) VIN Number
    h) Email address
    i) Residential Address
    j) Contact Number
    k) Full ID/Passport number (Can be confirmed on its own)

If speaking to a 3rd party who knows & is in contact with the client, leave the following message:
“Sir/madam kindly advise Mr/Mrs/Miss/Dr {Client’s Full Name} that Cartrack’s Attorneys called regarding an outstanding Cartrack account. Please advise Mr/Mrs/Miss/Dr {Client’s Full Name} to contact Viljoen Attorneys urgently on 010 140 0085 to resolve this outstanding matter.”  

3. Client Details Update   
”Before we proceed with the call, please verify the following contact details.”

    a) Direct cell phone number
    b) Alternative Contact number (e.g. Work landline, secondary cellphone)
    c) Email address
    d) Residential Address
    e) Next of kin details/Emergency Contact

4. Reason for call
“{Client Name/Sir/Ma’am}, be advised that Cartrack has handed over your account to us as Viljoen Attorneys due to non-payment, your total arrears are [Balance] and we are now acting on their behalf to recover these outstanding arrears. Do you acknowledge this debt?”
If “Yes,” Proceed to negotiation. 
If “No,” – Probe for reason, then resolve the query before proceeding to negotiation.

5. Negotiation 
    a) SETTLEMENT
Active Accounts
“Please take note that Cartrack services to you remain suspended and you have been/will be listed as a default payer on your credit profile due to your account being in arrears if you wish to have the listing cleared after settling your account, there will be an applicable fee of R1800. Should your vehicle be stolen or hijacked, you may be charged a fee of up to R25 000 for us to send out a recovery team.”

Cancelled Accounts
“Please take note that you have been/will be listed as a default payer on your credit profile due to your account being in arrears, making it difficult for you to get approved when you apply for credit. If you wish to have the listing cleared after settling your account, there will be an applicable fee of R1800.”
    b) DISCOUNT OFFER (Refer to the Discount Mandate at the end of the script)
“To prevent further legal steps from being taken, we are offering you a [Discount Percentage] settlement discount which requires you to pay [Discounted amount] once-off within this current month of [Current month] to settle the total arrears. Are you in a position to take up this discounted settlement offer?”
If “Yes,” – Proceed to provide Education & Capture PTP.  
If “No,” – Negotiate for a suitable instalment arrangement.

    c) INSTALMENT ARRANGEMENT 

If the contract is still ACTIVE = Add the Subscription amount. If CANCELLED = Add Cancellation Value. 
“Seeing that you are not in a position to settle the arrears once off with our discount offer, we can assist you to bring your account up to date by paying this discounted amount as follows:

[Instalment] to be debited for the next 2 months on your suitable debit date to cater for this discounted settlement balance of [Amount] plus your monthly subscription of [sub] 
Recurring Instalments (Guidelines)
    • Balance: R2000 or less
A minimum of [Instalment] is to be debited for the next 3 months on your suitable debit date to cater for this discounted settlement balance of [Amount] plus your monthly subscription of [sub]
    • Balance: R2000 – R4000
A minimum of [Instalment] is to be debited for the next 4 months on your suitable debit date to cater for this discounted settlement balance of [Amount] plus your monthly subscription of [sub]
    • Balance: R4000 & above
A minimum of [Instalment] is to be debited for the next 6 months on your suitable debit date to cater for this discounted settlement balance of [Amount] plus your monthly subscription of [sub]
Negotiate with the client to agree on a suitable instalment arrangement based on a discounted settlement amount.
If the client has a query, resolve it effectively
If “Yes,” – Proceed to provide Education & Capture PTP  
If “No,” – provide Education (particularly the consequence of non-payment)

6. Education (Benefits of paying Account)
Active Accounts
    • Settlement: - “By settling these arrears, you ensure that once the payment has been received, your account will be up to date and any pending legal action will be stopped. Your account will be handed back to Cartrack where all services to you will be re-instated.”
    • Instalment Arrangement: - “By paying [PTP Amount] for the next [number of instalments] months you ensure that pending legal action is put on hold as long as the payments are received as per this arrangement. After these instalments have all been paid, your account will be up to date and handed back to Cartrack where all services will be re-instated.”
Cancelled Accounts
    • Settlement: - “By settling these arrears, you ensure that once the payment has been received, your account will be closed and any pending legal action will be stopped. We will provide you with a paid-up letter as proof that you no longer owe Cartrack.”
    • Instalment Arrangement: - “By paying [PTP Amount] for the next [number of instalments] months you ensure that pending legal action is put on hold as long as the payments are received as per this arrangement. After these instalments have all been paid, your account will be closed and we will provide you with a paid-up letter as proof that you no longer owe Cartrack.”

7. Promise to Pay
“For your convenience can we debit this amount from your account today?”
    a) Settlement
If “Yes,” proceed to load a settlement (Preferably via the Cartrack Payment Portal)
If “No,” - “{Client Name/Sir/Ma’am}, please provide me with your salary date and confirm your Bank account details in which your salary gets paid.”

If still the same: Load Debicheck PTP If they have changed: Update the bank details on CAMS then load Debicheck PTP
    b) Instalment Arrangement
Load Debicheck for an instalment arrangement on the client’s preferred payment date.

8. Debicheck (Not applicable to same-day debit orders captured before 2 pm on the day)
“You will receive an authentication request to your cellphone from your bank to approve this Debicheck. {Client}, please approve this request as soon as you receive it to ensure we have a confirmed debit order arrangement. Also, note that there is an applicable R10 resubmission fee so the full amount to be debited will be {PTP Amount + R10}.”

Remember to check Bank detail errors and ensure they get fixed before loading the Debit order arrangement.

9. Cartrack Payment Portal
If the client will not pay via debit order, send them the link for the Cartrack payment portal. Advise of the available options & explain the payment process to the client.

“For your convenience, an SMS has been sent to you with a Cartrack payment portal link. Please click the PAY HERE button. It will take you to a payment page where you can edit the amount to the {Amount} that we agreed on. Once you have entered the amount we have agreed on, please click on CONFIRM & NEXT and you will see the different payment methods i.e. (Card Payment/Ozow/CapitecPay/Pay@).”

You can send the link on the spot, on CAMS if the client has not received the link.

“Please select that option that you prefer using and click CONFIRM & NEXT.”

Take the client through the steps for the selected payment method.

“Your payment as per the agreed arrangement has now been completed. You will get a pop-up showing you the total remaining arrears and asking you to arrange for the next payment to be made. Please edit the amount you will pay & the date, which must be within the next 30 days. You will receive an SMS reminder with a payment link a day before the date you have selected. You will click on the link and proceed to make the payment as you did now. Should you require any assistance, click on the WhatsApp icon & one of our friendly agents will assist you with completing the payment.”

10. Consequences of non-payment)
“Should you not honour the agreement to rectify this account, we will proceed to issue you with a notice of intent to summons followed by a letter of demand giving you 10 days to make the payment. Failure to make payment thereafter will result in us proceeding with actual summons through the sheriff of court.”

11. Referrals
“Do you know anyone who would be interested in Cartrack's services? Should they sign up and have a unit fitted on their vehicle after you have referred them, Cartrack will give you 2 months free on your subscription.”

If YES, ask the client to provide you with their contact details and action referral on the referral portal.
12. Further Assistance
“{Client Name} is there anything else you would like me to assist you with concerning your account?"

If YES, assist accordingly.

13. ADD A CLEAR & DETAILED NOTE (If applicable) & DISPOSITION CORRECTLY
14. Cancellation Declaration
If a client wants to cancel, establish the contract to be cancelled and the reason for the cancellation. Advise of the cancellation value (If applicable), and that you will escalate the cancellation request to the relevant department (Client services). 
Log a cancellation ticket on the Helpdesk (If applicable). Failure to do so will result in the call being zero-rated.
“Please be advised that the cancellation fee on your account is {Cancellation Value} and in total, you will need to pay {Total Balance} for the account to be paid up. I have logged a ticket with our cancellations department & they will contact you shortly to assist with your cancellation request and inform you of the applicable terms & conditions.”
